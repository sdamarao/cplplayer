How to Apply Standard iBullets in XMetaL

  • bullet-type [SELECT]

    • decimal (1 – 7)

    • folder

    • key

  • interactive click

  • custom-bullet-name [IMAGE] (see note)

  • interactive click

    7. comm.tip

    8. comment
